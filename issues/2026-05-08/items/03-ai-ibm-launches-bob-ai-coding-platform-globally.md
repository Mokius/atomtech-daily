# 🤖 IBM Launches Bob AI Coding Platform Globally with 80,000 Internal Users

> **🤖 Artificial Intelligence** · 🟡 Medium · 2026-05-08

---

## 📋 Summary
IBM has announced the global external launch of Bob, its AI-powered software development platform that combines multi-model routing with mandatory human checkpoints to keep AI-generated code production-safe. Bob started with 100 internal IBM users in summer 2025 and scaled to over 80,000 employees before opening to external customers. The platform writes, tests, and reviews code across the full development cycle.

## 💡 Why It Matters
Bob represents a mature, enterprise-grade approach to AI coding: rather than raw code generation, it enforces human review gates at critical stages — addressing the top concern enterprises have about shipping AI-generated code unsupervised. IBM's internal scale-up to 80,000 users before external launch provides genuine production validation most competing products lack.

## 🔍 What Is This?
Bob is an AI platform that intelligently routes coding tasks to the best model for the job (multi-model routing) and pauses at key decision points for a human to approve or redirect — rather than generating entire codebases autonomously and hoping for the best.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
The human-checkpoint model Bob uses may become an industry standard for regulated industries (finance, healthcare, government) where fully autonomous AI coding carries unacceptable liability risk.
