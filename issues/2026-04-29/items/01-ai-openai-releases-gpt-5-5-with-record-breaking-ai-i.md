# 🤖 OpenAI Releases GPT-5.5 with Record-Breaking AI Index Score

> **🤖 Artificial Intelligence** · 🔴 High · 2026-04-29

---

## 📋 Summary
OpenAI announced GPT-5.5 on April 23, 2026 — just six weeks after the incremental GPT-5.4 update. The new model immediately claimed the top position on the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index with a record score of 60. GPT-5.5 brings significant advances in coding, computer use, and deep research workflows.

## 💡 Why It Matters
GPT-5.5 sets a new benchmark ceiling for frontier AI capabilities, accelerating the pace at which AI systems can handle complex, multi-step technical tasks. The rapid six-week release cycle signals that OpenAI is shifting from major annual releases to continuous capability improvements. Enterprises already integrating GPT-5 will likely see meaningful productivity gains with this drop-in upgrade.

## 🔍 What Is This?
GPT-5.5 is the latest large language model from OpenAI — the company behind ChatGPT. Think of it as a smarter, faster version of the AI assistant millions already use, particularly better at writing code, navigating computers, and doing in-depth research autonomously.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
The six-week cadence of releases suggests we may see GPT-5.6 or early GPT-6 previews by summer 2026. Competitors like DeepSeek, Anthropic, and Google are already racing to close the benchmark gap.
