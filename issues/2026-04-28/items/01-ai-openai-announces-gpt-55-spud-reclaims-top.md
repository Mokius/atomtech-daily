# 🤖 OpenAI Announces GPT-5.5 'Spud', Immediately Reclaims Top AI Benchmark Spot

> **🤖 Artificial Intelligence** · 🔴 High · 2026-04-28

---

## 📋 Summary
OpenAI released GPT-5.5, internally codenamed "Spud," just six weeks after the incremental GPT-5.4 update. The model immediately topped the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index with a record score of 60. GPT-5.5 is now rolling out to paid subscribers — Plus, Pro, Business, and Enterprise — through ChatGPT and Codex.

## 💡 Why It Matters
The rapid cadence of releases underscores the breakneck pace of frontier AI competition in 2026, with OpenAI clearly feeling pressure from DeepSeek, Google, and Anthropic. A record benchmark score signals meaningful real-world capability gains, particularly in coding and complex reasoning tasks that enterprise buyers care about most.

## 🔍 What Is This?
GPT-5.5 is OpenAI's newest large language model — a powerful AI system that understands and generates text, code, and more. "Spud" is the internal project name. It essentially makes the AI smarter and faster across a wide range of tasks compared to its predecessor.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
With DeepSeek V4 hot on its heels and Google's Gemini 3.x series competitive, expect OpenAI to continue accelerating release cycles. Agentic capabilities and multimodal reasoning improvements are likely focal points for GPT-5.6 or a successor within months.
