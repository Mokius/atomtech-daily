# 🤖 DeepSeek Releases V4 Preview with 1.6 Trillion Parameter MoE Model

> **🤖 Artificial Intelligence** · 🔴 High · 2026-04-28

---

## 📋 Summary
Chinese AI startup DeepSeek released a preview of its V4 model on April 24, featuring two Mixture-of-Experts variants: DeepSeek-V4-Pro at 1.6 trillion parameters (49B activated) and DeepSeek-V4-Flash at 284B parameters (13B activated). Both models support a one-million token context window and are open source. Pricing starts at $0.14 per million input tokens for Flash and $1.74 for Pro.

## 💡 Why It Matters
DeepSeek continues to challenge the assumption that frontier AI requires Western tech giants' resources. V4-Pro achieves near-frontier performance at a fraction of the compute cost, with inference requiring only 27% of the FLOPs of its predecessor at 1M-token context. This puts sustained pressure on both OpenAI and Anthropic's pricing and open-source positioning.

## 🔍 What Is This?
A Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) model activates only a subset of its total parameters for each task — like a firm where specialists are called in only when needed. DeepSeek V4-Pro has 1.6 trillion total parameters but uses only 49 billion at any moment, making it highly efficient despite its massive scale.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
With V4 still in preview and the full release imminent, expect benchmarks to tighten further against GPT-5.5. DeepSeek's continued open-source releases will also fuel a wave of fine-tuned community models across Hugging Face and GitHub.
