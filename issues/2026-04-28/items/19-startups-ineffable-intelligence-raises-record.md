# 🚀 Ineffable Intelligence Raises Record 1.1 Billion Dollar Seed Round to Pursue Superintelligence

> **🚀 Startups** · 🔴 High · 2026-04-28

---

## 📋 Summary
Ineffable Intelligence, a London-based AI startup founded by former Google DeepMind researcher David Silver (known for AlphaGo and AlphaZero), raised $1.1 billion in seed funding at a $5.1 billion valuation — the largest seed round in startup history. The funding was led by Nvidia and Google, with the company pursuing superintelligence research focused on reinforcement learning and autonomous reasoning systems.

## 💡 Why It Matters
A $1.1B seed round is unprecedented in startup history by a substantial margin. The fact that Nvidia and Google are co-leading reflects both the strategic importance of foundational AI research and the competitive dynamics of the frontier AI race. David Silver's pedigree — as the lead researcher on AlphaGo, AlphaZero, and AlphaStar — means Ineffable Intelligence is not speculative: it is a bet on one of the most credentialed reinforcement learning researchers in the world.

## 🔍 What Is This?
David Silver is famous for creating AlphaGo, the AI that beat world champion Go players, and AlphaZero, which mastered chess, Go, and shogi from scratch by playing against itself. Ineffable Intelligence is his new company, pursuing the next frontier: AI systems that can reason and learn autonomously at a level beyond current models. The $1.1 billion raised at seed stage — before a product exists — reflects investors' conviction in the founding team's track record.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
With Nvidia and Google as lead investors, Ineffable Intelligence will have privileged access to compute infrastructure and research collaboration. The reinforcement learning focus suggests a differentiated technical approach from current dominant paradigms — and Silver's track record of breakthrough results means the research output is worth watching closely regardless of commercialization timeline.
