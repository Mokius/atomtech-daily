# 🚀 Sierra AI Raises $950M at $15.8B Valuation Led by Tiger Global and GV

> **🚀 Startups** · 🔴 High · 2026-05-07

---

## 📋 Summary
Sierra, an AI startup founded by former Salesforce co-CEO Bret Taylor, has raised $950 million in new funding at a $15.8 billion post-money valuation. The round was led by Tiger Global and GV (Google Ventures), nearly doubling Sierra's previous funding total. The capital will be used to expand Sierra's enterprise AI chat agent platform, which serves as the customer-facing AI interface for major brand and retail clients.

## 💡 Why It Matters
A $15.8 billion valuation for an enterprise AI agent company signals that investors see AI-powered customer engagement as one of the most valuable use cases of the current AI wave. Sierra's rapid valuation growth — from startup to $15.8B in a compressed timeline — reflects extraordinary investor conviction in the enterprise AI agent market.

## 🔍 What Is This?
Sierra builds AI chat agents that act as the customer service representatives for large brands. When you interact with a smart chatbot on a major retailer's website, it might be powered by Sierra's technology. The company just raised nearly $1 billion to expand this business.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
At $15.8B, Sierra's valuation sets a market benchmark for enterprise AI agent companies. Expect competitor consolidation, accelerated hiring, and possible IPO planning within 18-24 months as Sierra works to establish a dominant market position.
