# 🤖 OpenAI Releases GPT-5.5, Tops AI Benchmarks with Record Score of 60

> **🤖 Artificial Intelligence** · 🔴 High · 2026-04-30

---

## 📋 Summary
OpenAI released GPT-5.5 on April 23, 2026, just six weeks after the incremental GPT-5.4 update. The model immediately claimed the number one spot on the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index with a record score of 60. GPT-5.5 demonstrates significantly improved capabilities in coding, computer use, and deep research tasks.

## 💡 Why It Matters
GPT-5.5 sets a new performance ceiling for commercially available AI, pushing rivals to accelerate their own roadmaps. Its gains in autonomous coding and research task completion suggest that AI agents are moving from demo-ware to production-ready. The rapid six-week iteration cadence signals OpenAI is in an all-out competitive sprint.

## 🔍 What Is This?
GPT-5.5 is OpenAI's latest large language model — software trained on vast amounts of text and data that can answer questions, write code, conduct research, and reason through complex problems. Think of it as a significantly upgraded version of the AI assistant already used by millions worldwide.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
If OpenAI maintains its rapid release cadence, GPT-5.6 or a successor could arrive before summer. The benchmark race will intensify as Google DeepMind, Anthropic, and Chinese labs respond — accelerating capabilities across the entire industry.
