# 🚀 Ineffable Intelligence Raises $1.1B Seed Round at $5.1B Valuation, Founded by DeepMind's David Silver

> **🚀 Startups** · 🔴 High · 2026-04-30

---

## 📋 Summary
Ineffable Intelligence, a London-based AI startup founded by former DeepMind researcher David Silver — the architect of AlphaGo and AlphaZero — has secured $1.1 billion in seed funding at a $5.1 billion valuation. The company is developing next-generation reinforcement learning systems aimed at superhuman planning and decision-making. The round represents one of the largest seed investments in European tech history.

## 💡 Why It Matters
David Silver's track record is unparalleled in AI: AlphaGo's defeat of world Go champion Lee Sedol in 2016 was a defining moment in AI history, and AlphaZero generalized that approach to chess, shogi, and beyond. His departure from DeepMind to found Ineffable Intelligence signals that the frontiers of reinforcement learning may be where the next major AI breakthrough occurs.

## 🔍 What Is This?
Reinforcement learning is an AI training approach where systems learn by trial and error, receiving rewards for good decisions. It's how AlphaGo learned to play Go better than any human. Ineffable Intelligence is applying this approach to broader real-world planning and decision-making challenges, potentially extending its reach far beyond games.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
A $1.1B seed round gives Ineffable Intelligence runway to train large-scale RL systems and compete with the largest frontier AI labs. Watch for research publications and potential applications in domains like robotics, logistics optimization, and scientific discovery — areas where superhuman planning could have transformative impact.
