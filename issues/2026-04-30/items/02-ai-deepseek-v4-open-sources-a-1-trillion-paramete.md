# 🤖 DeepSeek V4 Open-Sources a 1-Trillion-Parameter Model Trained for $5.2M

> **🤖 Artificial Intelligence** · 🔴 High · 2026-04-30

---

## 📋 Summary
Chinese AI startup DeepSeek released a preview of its V4 large language model on April 24, 2026, featuring a one-trillion-parameter Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) architecture with fully open weights under the Apache 2.0 license. The V4 Flash and V4 Pro variants deliver top-tier performance on coding benchmarks and major advances in reasoning and agentic tasks. The model reportedly cost only $5.2 million to train, competitive with US frontier models costing hundreds of millions.

## 💡 Why It Matters
DeepSeek V4's efficiency upends the assumption that frontier-level AI requires billion-dollar training budgets. Releasing under Apache 2.0 means any developer, company, or researcher can run, fine-tune, or commercialize the model freely. This could dramatically democratize access to top-tier AI capabilities and put further pressure on the pricing of proprietary models.

## 🔍 What Is This?
DeepSeek is a Chinese AI lab that shocked the tech world in early 2025 with its highly efficient models. A Mixture-of-Experts model activates only a fraction of its parameters for each query — like consulting a team of specialists rather than asking everyone at once — making it fast and cheap to run despite its massive total size.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
The cost-efficiency race in AI training is accelerating. If a trillion-parameter model can be trained for $5.2 million today, the next generation may be accessible for far less, fundamentally reshaping who can build and deploy frontier AI.
