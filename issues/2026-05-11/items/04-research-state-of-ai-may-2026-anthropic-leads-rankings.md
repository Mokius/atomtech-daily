# 🔬 State of AI May 2026: Anthropic Leads Model Rankings Ahead of xAI and Google

> **🔬 AI Research** · 🟡 Medium · 2026-05-11

---

## 📋 Summary
The May 2026 State of AI report from Air Street Press reveals Anthropic has taken the lead in AI model rankings, closely trailed by xAI, Google, and OpenAI. Major model releases this month include GPT-5.5 from OpenAI, Gemma 4 31B from Google, and updated Claude models from Anthropic. The report highlights that Chinese models from DeepSeek and Alibaba lag only modestly behind the leading Western models, indicating continued competitive pressure from Chinese AI labs.

## 💡 Why It Matters
Anthropic leading the overall model rankings is a significant shift — OpenAI and Google had long dominated performance benchmarks. The competitive parity between Western and Chinese AI models raises important geopolitical considerations around AI development and deployment. The release cadence of major models is accelerating, suggesting the AI capability race is far from settled.

## 🔍 What Is This?
AI model rankings compare different companies' AI systems on standardized tests covering reasoning, coding, mathematics, and language tasks. Air Street Press produces regular "State of AI" reports tracking who is ahead in the global AI race — it's like a leaderboard for AI companies.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
The narrowing gap between top models suggests we are approaching a period where model quality differences become less decisive, and factors like pricing, latency, and enterprise integration become the primary competitive battlegrounds. The Chinese AI labs' continued progress will likely intensify geopolitical debates around AI access and export controls.
