# 🤖 NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Nano Omni: Open-Source Multimodal AI Model Arrives on OCI

> **🤖 Artificial Intelligence** · 🔴 High · 2026-05-11

---

## 📋 Summary
NVIDIA's Nemotron 3 Nano Omni is now available on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) Enterprise AI as a fully open-source, multimodal model capable of reasoning across video, audio, images, and text in a single unified system. The model represents a significant step forward in multimodal AI, combining multiple modalities that previously required separate specialized models. Its open-source nature makes it accessible to researchers and enterprises building customized AI applications.

## 💡 Why It Matters
Fully open-source multimodal models are rare — most frontier multimodal systems are locked behind proprietary APIs. Nemotron 3 Nano Omni gives developers and researchers the ability to inspect, fine-tune, and deploy a capable multimodal system without vendor lock-in. The ability to process video, audio, images, and text in one model dramatically simplifies AI pipeline architecture for applications requiring multiple media types.

## 🔍 What Is This?
A multimodal AI model can understand and reason about different types of data at once — not just text, but also images, audio, and video. NVIDIA, the leading chip maker for AI, built Nemotron 3 Nano Omni and released it as open-source, meaning anyone can freely use, modify, and build on top of it.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
As open-source multimodal models improve, the competitive advantage of proprietary systems will erode, accelerating AI adoption across industries. Expect to see Nemotron 3 Nano Omni fine-tuned for specific domains like medical imaging, video surveillance, and industrial inspection over the coming months.
