# DeepSeek Unveils V4 Series with 1M-Token Context and Hybrid Attention

> **🤖 Artificial Intelligence** · 🔴 High · 2026-04-27

---

## 📋 Summary
China’s DeepSeek released preview versions of its V4 flagship model series (V4 Flash and V4 Pro), featuring top-tier coding benchmarks and significant advances in reasoning and agentic tasks. The company introduced a Hybrid Attention Architecture that dramatically improves long-conversation memory and pushed its context window to 1 million tokens. The release arrives exactly one year after DeepSeek’s V2 disrupted Silicon Valley’s assumptions about AI development costs.

## 💡 Why It Matters
DeepSeek continues to prove that world-class AI models can be developed outside the US tech ecosystem, maintaining competitive pressure that forces the entire industry to improve. The 1M-token context window is practically transformative for developers building over large codebases, legal teams processing long contracts, and researchers analyzing extensive literature.

## 🔍 What Is This?
Context window refers to how much text an AI can read and remember in a single conversation. With 1 million tokens, DeepSeek V4 can hold the equivalent of several full-length novels in active memory at once — vastly larger than most current models.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
The V4 Flash and Pro series are preview-only; general availability is expected within weeks. If released open-source, V4 will likely become the new baseline for self-hosted enterprise AI deployments globally.
