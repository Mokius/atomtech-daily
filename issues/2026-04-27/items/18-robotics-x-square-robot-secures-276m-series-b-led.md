# X Square Robot Secures $276M Series B Led by Xiaomi and Sequoia China

> **🦾 Robotics** · 🟡 Medium · 2026-04-27

---

## 📋 Summary
X Square Robot closed a $276 million Series B round on April 20, co-led by Xiaomi and Sequoia China, making it one of the most highly funded robotics startups in Asia. X Square focuses on general-purpose humanoid robots for industrial and logistics applications. The Xiaomi co-lead signals strategic intent to integrate robot hardware with consumer electronics ecosystems. The robotics sector has attracted over $2.25 billion in funding over the past 12 months.

## 💡 Why It Matters
Xiaomi’s co-lead investment is strategically significant — it connects consumer device distribution networks to robotics platforms, potentially accelerating enterprise and consumer deployment of humanoid robots. Sequoia China’s involvement adds credibility and an extensive portfolio support network.

## 🔍 What Is This?
A Series B is a later-stage funding round used to scale a startup that has already proven its product works. $276 million is an exceptionally large Series B, indicating strong investor confidence in X Square Robot’s technology and market opportunity.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
X Square is expected to announce commercial deployment partnerships with major logistics operators in China by Q3 2026. International expansion filings are anticipated within 12 months of this round.
