# OpenAI Releases GPT-5.5, Its Most Capable Model to Date

> **🤖 Artificial Intelligence** · 🔴 High · 2026-04-27

---

## 📋 Summary
OpenAI has officially released GPT-5.5, described as its smartest and most intuitive model yet, with significantly improved performance in coding, computer-use tasks, and deep research. The release is positioned as a key step toward OpenAI’s vision of building an AI super app that consolidates most productivity tasks into a single interface. GPT-5.5 shows marked improvements in agentic tasks — multi-step workflows where AI acts autonomously on behalf of users.

## 💡 Why It Matters
GPT-5.5 raises the capability ceiling for widely deployed AI yet again, with coding and research improvements that will directly affect developers, researchers, and knowledge workers. Its launch accelerates competitive pressure on Google, Anthropic, and Meta to ship next-generation models. The super-app framing signals OpenAI’s ambition to own a much larger slice of the daily productivity stack.

## 🔍 What Is This?
GPT-5.5 is OpenAI’s latest large language model — the AI engine powering ChatGPT. Think of it as the newest version of a very capable AI assistant that can write code, conduct research, and take actions on a computer on your behalf.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
Expect rapid adoption across enterprise software platforms and developer tools in Q2 2026. The super-app strategy, if successful, could consolidate workflows that currently require dozens of separate applications.
