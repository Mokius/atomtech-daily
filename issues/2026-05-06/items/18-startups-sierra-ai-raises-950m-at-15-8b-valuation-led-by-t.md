# 🚀 Sierra AI Raises $950M at $15.8B Valuation Led by Tiger Global and GV

> **🚀 Startups & Funding** · 🔴 High · 2026-05-06

---

## 📋 Summary
Sierra, the AI startup co-founded by former Salesforce chairman Bret Taylor and former Google executive Clay Bavor, raised $950 million in new funding at a $15.8 billion post-money valuation. The round was led by Tiger Global and GV (Google Ventures). Founded three years ago, Sierra focuses on enterprise AI agents for customer engagement and business automation, and the raise represents one of the largest single AI funding rounds of 2026.

## 💡 Why It Matters
Sierra's $15.8 billion valuation at roughly three years old signals that enterprise AI agent companies — particularly those with strong founding teams and enterprise traction — are commanding exceptional valuations. The involvement of both Tiger Global and GV as co-leads reflects broad institutional conviction. Bret Taylor's enterprise credibility from Salesforce and Google gives Sierra significant customer access that pure technology startups lack.

## 🔍 What Is This?
Sierra is a startup that builds AI agents — automated software assistants — designed to handle customer service and business operations for large companies. They just raised $950 million, which values the company at $15.8 billion, making it one of the most valuable AI startups in the world.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
With $950 million in fresh capital, Sierra has the resources to aggressively expand its enterprise customer base, hire engineering talent, and potentially make acquisitions. The company will face intensifying competition from Salesforce Einstein, ServiceNow AI, and OpenAI's enterprise agent offerings.
