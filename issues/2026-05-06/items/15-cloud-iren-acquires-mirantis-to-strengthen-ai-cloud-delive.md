# ☁️ IREN Acquires Mirantis to Strengthen AI Cloud Delivery and Kubernetes Capabilities

> **☁️ Cloud & Infrastructure** · 🟡 Medium · 2026-05-06

---

## 📋 Summary
IREN Limited announced on May 5, 2026 that it has signed a definitive agreement to acquire Mirantis, Inc., a cloud infrastructure and Kubernetes orchestration provider serving over 1,500 enterprise customers globally. Mirantis is a founding Independent Software Vendor partner of the NVIDIA AI Cloud Ready Initiative. The acquisition strengthens IREN's ability to deploy, manage, and operate AI compute infrastructure for enterprise customers.

## 💡 Why It Matters
Mirantis has built significant Kubernetes expertise and enterprise customer relationships over many years, and its role as an NVIDIA AI Cloud Ready ISV partner makes it strategically valuable as enterprises race to build AI-ready infrastructure. For IREN, this acquisition accelerates its transition from compute provider to full-stack AI cloud delivery platform. The deal also signals continued consolidation in the cloud infrastructure market.

## 🔍 What Is This?
Mirantis is a company that helps businesses run their applications in the cloud using Kubernetes, the standard system for managing containerized software. IREN is acquiring them to combine their AI compute hardware with Mirantis's software expertise, creating a more complete AI cloud offering for enterprise customers.

## 🔭 Looking Ahead
The combined IREN-Mirantis entity will compete more directly with hyperscalers for AI infrastructure managed services contracts. Expect the integration to focus on NVIDIA GPU cluster management and Kubernetes-based AI workload orchestration for enterprise deployments.
