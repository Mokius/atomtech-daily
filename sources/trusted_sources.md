# Trusted Sources Registry

This file lists the authoritative sources used for news collection.
The automation prioritizes these domains when searching and validating stories.

## Blocked Sources (never use)

- Content farms without bylines
- Sites with >50% advertising content
- Sources known for misinformation
- Paywalled sites without public summaries
- Pure social media posts without backing article
